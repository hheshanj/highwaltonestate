Growing Nature, Cultivating Legacy

Situated in the Heart of Matale, Highwolton Estate is a flourshing platform where the richness of nature blends seamlessly with innovation. From towering trees to thriving crops our estate reflects our unwavering commitment to sustainable growth and excellence.

Our Vision

To nuture a future where the timeless bond between Land and People thrives. By preserving the essence of nature's gifts and embracing new opportunities, we aspire to grow not only in what we cultivate but in enduring the legacy we leave for generations to come.


Our Mission

At Highwalton Estate, our mission is to cultivate with care and gorw with purpose. We are dedicated to nurturing both the land and our community, we aim to create lasting connections, offering experiences that bring the people close to the land in meaningful ways.

A Journey of Growth, Heritage, and New Horizons.

Highwolton Estate, the birthplace of Sri Lanka's first coffee plant, remains deeply connected to its agricultural heritage. This legacy drives its commitment to cultivating a diverse range of trees and crops, enhancing the estate’s ecosystem while preserving its historical significance.

Highwolton Estate is expanding beyond cultivation, offering visitors opportunities to connect with nature and heritage. Through carefully designed ventures, it fosters growth, community, and sustainability.
