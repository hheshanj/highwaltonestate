Growing Nature, Cultivating Legacy

Located in the heart of Matale, Highwalton Estate is a thriving hub where nature's richness meets innovation. With lush trees and flourishing crops, we embody a commitment to sustainable growth and excellence.

Our Vision

Nurturing a future where the bond between land and people thrives, we preserve nature’s gifts while embracing new opportunities, ensuring growth and a lasting legacy for future generations.


Our Mission

Highwalton Estate's mission is to cultivate with care and grow with purpose, fostering connections between land and community through meaningful experiences.

A Journey of Growth, Heritage, and New Horizons.

Highwalton Estate, the birthplace of Sri Lanka's first coffee plant, remains deeply connected to its agricultural heritage. This legacy drives its commitment to cultivating a diverse range of trees and crops, enhancing the estate’s ecosystem while preserving its historical significance.

The Estate is expanding beyond cultivation, offering visitors opportunities to connect with nature and heritage. Through carefully designed ventures, it fosters growth, community, and sustainability.
